'''TRS-80 BASIC''' is the collective term for the dialects of [[BASIC]] created for the [[wp:TRS-80|TRS-80]] series of computers. The original version that shipped in the Model I ROM, [[wp:Level I BASIC|Level I BASIC]], was an extension of [[Tiny BASIC]] developed internally at Tandy. Later versions – Level II BASIC, TRSDOS Disk BASIC, Level III BASIC, and ColorBASIC – were developed by Microsoft.
